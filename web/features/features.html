Features

Web DNA Analyzer

Analyze DNA sequences directly through your web browser with our powerful web interface. Upload FASTA/FASTQ files and get instant analysis with customizable parameters.

Start and Stop Codons

Indicate the start and stop of a protein. There are 20 different amino acids. A protein consists of one or more chains of amino acids (called polypeptides) whose sequence is encoded in a gene.

High Coverage Regions

Regions of a DNA genome that code for a protein and have a relatively high proportion of guanine and cytosine nucleotides to the 4 nucleotide bases (45-60% GC-content).

Neurodevelopmental Disorders

A group of disorders, usually characterized by longer genes, that affect the development of the brain and nervous system.

Transcription Factors

A protein that binds to DNA and regulates the transcription of genes. These proteins are responsible for the regulation of gene expression.
