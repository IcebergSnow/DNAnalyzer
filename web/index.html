The Problem with Traditional DNA Testing

Your most personal data is at risk and insights are costly.

Privacy Risks

Competitors require uploading your DNA to their servers, risking breaches (like 23andMe's 6.9M user hack) and data sharing without explicit consent.

High Costs

Sequencing costs ~$100, yet providers charge up to $600+ for reports, making deep insights inaccessible for many.

Lack of Control

Once uploaded, you lose control over how your sensitive genetic data is used, stored, or potentially sold.

DNAnalyzer's Solution: Privacy & Power

How DNAnalyzer Works

Simple, secure, and powerful analysis in three steps.

1. Upload Securely

Upload your DNA file (FASTA, FASTQ, VCF etc.) directly in the browser. Your file is processed locally and never leaves your computer.

2. Choose Analyses

Select from basic analysis (free) or unlock advanced AI-powered reports (Premium) covering health, traits, ancestry, and more.

3. Get Instant Insights

Receive comprehensive, interactive reports generated entirely on your device within minutes. Explore your results privately.
