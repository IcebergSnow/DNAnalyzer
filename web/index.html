Why Choose DNAnalyzer?

Gain unparalleled insights with cutting-edge technology, while ensuring your sensitive data remains secure and private.

Absolute Privacy

Your genetic data never leaves your device. All computation happens locally, eliminating cloud-based privacy risks.

AI-Driven Insights

Go beyond traditional methods. Our ML models identify complex patterns, ORFs, GC-rich regions, and potential promoter elements with high accuracy.

Fast & Accessible

Get results quickly without specialized hardware. Accessible to researchers, students, and enthusiasts alike via web and desktop apps.

Simple Steps to Insight

Analyze your DNA sequences securely in just three easy steps.

1

Upload Securely

Provide your DNA sequence (FASTA/FASTQ). It stays entirely on your device, ensuring complete privacy.

2

Select Analysis

Choose from various ML-powered analyses like ORF detection, GC content, promoter identification, and more.

3

Get Results

Receive comprehensive, visualized results and downloadable reports directly in the application.

Powerful Analysis Capabilities

Go beyond basic sequence stats with our suite of advanced analytical tools.

Open Reading Frame ID

Identify potential protein-coding regions by scanning for start/stop codons across all reading frames.

GC-Rich Region Detection

Locate high GC-content areas, often indicative of gene-dense or regulatory regions.

Promoter Element ID

Scan for core promoter motifs (TATA box, BRE, etc.) to pinpoint potential gene start sites.

Protein Translation

Translate identified ORFs into their corresponding amino acid sequences.

Codon Usage Analysis

Analyze codon frequency within sequences or identified ORFs.

Comprehensive Reporting

Generate detailed reports with visualizations for easy interpretation and sharing.
